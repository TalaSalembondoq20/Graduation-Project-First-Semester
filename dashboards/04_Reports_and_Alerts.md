# Reports and Alerts

## Description

The reports and alerts feature provides operational notifications and summary reports for the pyrolysis plant.

## Alert Examples

Possible alerts include:

- Reactor delay
- High buffer accumulation
- Maintenance required
- Abnormal temperature
- Low output level
- Batch completion
- Machine downtime

## Reports

The monitoring application can generate simple reports such as:

- Daily production report
- Reactor utilization report
- Product output report
- Maintenance report
- Environmental recovery summary
- Financial performance summary

## Decision Support Role

Reports and alerts help operators respond faster to system issues. They also support management decisions by summarizing important operational and financial information.
