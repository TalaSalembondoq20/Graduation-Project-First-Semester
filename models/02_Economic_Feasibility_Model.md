# Economic Feasibility Model

## Purpose

The economic feasibility model evaluates whether the proposed small-scale waste tyre pyrolysis system is financially attractive.

## Cost Structure

The model includes:

- Capital expenditure (CAPEX)
- Monthly operating cost (OPEX)
- Daily operating cost
- Product revenue
- Net daily profit
- Monthly net profit
- Annual net profit

## Main Financial Indicators

The economic model evaluates:

- Net daily profit
- Annual net profit
- Payback period
- Return on investment
- Break-even behavior

## Expected Scenario

The expected scenario assumes a capital investment of approximately 700,000 JD and an expected net daily profit of approximately 2,217 JD/day. These assumptions are used to estimate the expected annual profit, payback period, and ROI.

## Decision Role

The economic model helps determine whether the plant can be financially feasible under realistic operating conditions.
