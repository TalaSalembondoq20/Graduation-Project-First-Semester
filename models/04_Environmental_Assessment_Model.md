# Environmental Assessment Model

## Purpose

The environmental assessment model evaluates the sustainability contribution of the waste tyre pyrolysis system.

## Assessment Areas

The environmental model focuses on:

- Waste tyre reduction
- Resource recovery
- Product recovery
- Energy recovery
- Avoided landfill or uncontrolled disposal
- Reuse of gas as an internal energy source

## Product Streams

The main recovered products include:

- Pyrolysis oil
- Carbon black
- Gas
- Light fuel fraction
- Steel wires

## Sustainability Role

The proposed system supports circular economy principles by converting waste tyres into useful products instead of leaving them in landfills or stockpiles.
